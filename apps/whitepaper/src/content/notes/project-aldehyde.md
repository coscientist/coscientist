# Project Aldehyde

Project Aldehyde was [Sunghyun Cho](./sunghyun-cho)'s early attempt to build a personal [digital brain](./digital-brain) that could grow with his writing and research.

## Outcome

It led to the creation of [Extracranial](./extracranial) and later ideas behind [Coscientist](./coscientist).
