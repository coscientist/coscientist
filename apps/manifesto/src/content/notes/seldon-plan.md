---
title: Seldon Plan
description: Hari Seldon's long-range strategy to shorten a dark age
---

The Seldon Plan is Hari Seldon's long-range strategy to shorten a post-collapse dark age. Derived from [psychohistory](./psychohistory), it treats history as something you can steer by designing institutions and incentives that shape long-run trajectories.

In Asimov's story, the [Encyclopedia Galactica](./encyclopedia-galactica) project is the nominal purpose and the public cover, while the deeper function is to seed a path out of the coming [dark night of the soul](./dark-night-of-the-soul).
