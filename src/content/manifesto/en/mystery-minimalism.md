The [Monolith](./monolith) teaches a design principle: power through restraint.
It communicates without symbols, instructs without words. This informs the visual
language of [Coscientist](./coscientist).

## The Principle

Remove everything that does not serve understanding. What remains gains weight.

- No decorative gradients
- No playful animations
- No unnecessary borders
- No redundant labels

The absence of ornamentation is itself meaningful. It signals: this is serious.
Pay attention.

### Color

Grayscale as foundation. Color appears only as signal: links, warnings, states.
When everything is colorful, nothing stands out. When color is rare, it demands
attention.

### Space

Generous margins and padding. The content breathes. Dense information walls
overwhelm; spaced information invites reading.

## The Paradox

Minimalism requires more design effort, not less. Every remaining element must
justify its existence. The [Monolith](./monolith) is featureless because every
feature was deliberately excluded.
