Sunghyun Cho is the builder of [Coscientist](./coscientist) and the author of essays that shaped its direction. His work connects personal knowledge tooling (for example [Creating Next-gen Digital Brains](./creating-next-gen-digital-brains) and [Extracranial](./extracranial)) with a broader concern: how to keep verification and agency intact in an AI-saturated environment.

Entry points include [ScienceOps](./scienceops), [AI Slop](./ai-slop), [Encyclopedia Meltdown](./encyclopedia-meltdown), and the system overview in [Coscientist](./coscientist).
