---
title: Definition Drift
description: When a term's meaning shifts across time or contexts
---

Definition drift is the gradual shift in meaning of a term across time, schools, or fields. It creates false agreement and false contradiction when the same word is used for different conceptsâ€”people appear to argue about conclusions when they are actually talking past one another.

In [Coscientist](./coscientist), drift is tracked explicitly by separating definition objects from claims. When a term changes meaning, old claims can be marked as meaning-lost or re-scoped instead of being silently mixed into a single "consensus."
